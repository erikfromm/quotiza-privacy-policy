Privacy Policy for Quotiza Sheet Connector

Last updated: November 25, 2024

Information We Collect

The Quotiza Sheet Connector extension collects:

  • Quotiza API credentials (token and account ID)
  • Google Sheets data that you explicitly choose to import

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Authenticating with Quotiza's API
  • Reading Google Spreadsheet data you choose to import
  • Importing product data into your Quotiza account

Data Storage

Your Quotiza credentials are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. No data is permanently stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

We interact with:

  • Google Sheets API - to read your spreadsheet data
  • Quotiza API - to import your products
